How we met

Andrea and David first met during a game of Ultimate Werewolf at a board game night in a Denver brewery. David convinced the other players that Andrea was the baddie when he was actually the werewolf himself. Little did he know that this was a preview of their future together. A couple weeks went by and David eventually asked Andrea to go ice skating. She had no idea it was a date at first, but after being informed of that fact she begrudgingly accepted his request. They went ice skating then Andrea insisted on meeting David's dog Sudo (who will be the ring-bearer in the wedding ceremony). Andrea fell in love with his dog immediately, but as the days go by she fell in love with David as well. As did he with her.
